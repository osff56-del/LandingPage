Vex Robotics Overview

The VEX Robotics Competition is an international program where students design, build, and program robots to compete in themed engineering challenges. Teams collaborate to solve problems, score points, and outperform opponents in fast-paced matches. Emphasizing STEM skills, teamwork, and innovation, the competition prepares participants for real-world engineering experiences. Students also develop critical thinking, communication, and project management abilities while engaging in a dynamic, hands-on environment that fosters creativity and inspires future careers in technology.
